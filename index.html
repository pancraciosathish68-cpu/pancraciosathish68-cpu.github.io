Rapid Cleanup - Photo Cleaner

Smart Storage Cleaner for Your Device

About the App

Rapid Cleanup is a powerful mobile tool designed to help users clean up unnecessary files, especially duplicate and similar photos, to free up storage space quickly and efficiently.

Key Features

  • Detect duplicate and similar photos
  • Quick photo cleanup in one tap
  • Smart file scanning
  • Storage optimization tools

How It Works

The app scans your device locally to identify unwanted files and provides suggestions for safe deletion. All operations are performed on-device.

Advertising
